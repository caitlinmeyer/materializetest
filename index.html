Hi, I'm Caitlin.

librarian/instructional designer/printmaker

I am a forward-looking medical librarian at Yale, where I coordinate our general education program and make user-friendly guides, tools and videos that help promote library resources and teach library skills. I'm newish to New England by way of Missouri, Tennessee, and, most recently, California. When I'm not working, you can find me kayaking, hiking, dancing to bluegrass music, or checking out a new brewery. Feel free to reach out.
