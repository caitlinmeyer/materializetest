Hi, I'm Caitlin.
I work at the intersection of design, libraries, and instruction.

I am a forward-looking librarian new to the field of medical librarianship at Yale. I'm interested in teaching, online learning, library tech and media production. I'm happiest when I'm making things that make a difference. I'm always interested in new projects, research, and conversation -- let's collaborate!
